The online is a cypher of ciphers. In vain the Tower has been rebuilt from the ruins of History. Surely the cypher of ciphers, God’s Gate, will be reached. But what was tragedy shall return as farce - who will have the Key? The key must be chosen wisely or not chosen at all. This lock can’t be picked but by the Word.

Cyphernetics is the autologist discipline structuring deception and deconstructing secrecy. The Fall into language is the Fall into history - the truth of deception, the deception of truth, the deception of deception, the truth of truth. Agency entails responsibility. Responsibility holds secrets.
